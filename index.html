cat ~/info.txt

Who can attend?
Anyone that is currently a college student or that has graduated in the past year is allowed to hack!
When and where?
April 9 - 10, 2016. Hollis Center at Stetson University. Use this map to find parking.
How much does it cost?
Attendance is completely free, we'll supply food, drinks, and energy products to keep you hacking!
What should I bring?
Your laptop, chargers, personal hygiene supplies, change of clothes, blanket, pillows, sleeping bag.
Do I have to stay the whole time?
We will not force you to stay, but suggest that you do. There are activities you do not want to miss! Please make sure all hacking is done at the event.
What if I am new to programming?
Beginners are always welcome! This is not only an event where you can win prizes, but also a learning experience for everyone.
How big can my team be?
You can have a team of two to four hackers, or you can hack by yourself.
Where is the fine print?
There is none! You just need to agree to the Major League Hacking Code of Conduct!
Have any more questions?
Send us a message: hack@stetson.edu.
